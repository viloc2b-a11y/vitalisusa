How VITALIS Portal works

We connect Houston's Hispanic community with modern clinical study options, at no cost, with support in their language.

1

Search your symptom

You do not need to be an expert or know a diagnosis. Just search what you are feeling today (for example, knee pain, sadness, high blood sugar). We show available Houston options that match your situation.

2

Complete the pre-screen

It is a short questionnaire (under 2 minutes). Your answers help us estimate if you may qualify. Everything is private and secure. You do not need health insurance or immigration status to participate.

3

We contact you in Spanish

If you qualify, someone from our team calls you. We speak your language and understand your culture. We explain everything clearly, answer your questions, and guide you through scheduling your first screening visit.

4

Access your medical option

At the medical site, you receive care from professionals. Many studies offer compensation for your time and transportation. Most importantly, you help future medicine work better for Hispanic communities too.
